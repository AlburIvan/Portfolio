Guide to reduce Android’s apk size

…The size does matter… at least in the PlayStore…

Punchline aside, the size of the APK does matter for numerous reasons, one of them being whats called Mobile Constraints, we live with them… limited bandwidth, disk space, smaller screen sizes, shorter battery life, less available memory, etc… Data is quite expensive, so to acquire and retain data-sensitive users you need to optimize every KB possible.
